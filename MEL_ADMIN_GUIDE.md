## 📝 **Step-by-Step: Creating a Blog Post**

### 1. **Login to Admin Dashboard**
   - Go to http://localhost:3003/admin
   - Enter your username and password

### 2. **Go to Blog Management**
   - Click on "Blog Management" in the navigation
   - Click the green "New Blog Post" button

### 3. **Fill in Blog Details**
   ```
   Title: "5 Tips for Perfect Laundry Results"
   Summary: "Discover our professional laundry secrets..."
   Content: "Write your full blog post here..."
   Author: "MEL Laundry Team" (or your name)
   Date: Today's date (auto-filled)
   Status: "Published" (to make it live) or "Draft" (to save privately)
   ```

### 4. **Publish Your Blog**
   - Click "Publish Blog Post" button
   - Your blog is now live on your website!
